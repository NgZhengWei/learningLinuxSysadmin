Resources for Becoming a Linux System Administrator
- No 1 book or course can cover all the knowledge.

Books:
- UNIX and Linux System Administration Handbook by Evi Nemeth
- The Practice of System and Network Administration by Thomas A. Limoncelli

Links:
- help.ubuntu.com/community/Servers (work through these guides as practice)
- serverfault.com (forum for questoins on servers)
- news.ycombinator.com (Hacker News - more on development and startup)

On Programming:
